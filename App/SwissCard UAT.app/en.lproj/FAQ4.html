You can find the last transactions that you made with your credit cards on the homepage. You will find all the transactions on the "Transactions" page. Here you can view all your transactions on a quarterly, monthly or weekly basis. It is also possible to filter the transactions according to specific cards if you have several credit cards with Swisscard.

If you want detailed information for a transaction, simply tap the corresponding transaction.

Please note that transactions that have been approved but not posted to your card account yet are not displayed.
