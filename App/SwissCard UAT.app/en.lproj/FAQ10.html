The expense tracker clearly shows you what you have used your card for in a specific period. Your purchases are shown according to your allocation based on the amount and as a percentage per defined category. The analysis is also presented clearly in a graph. You can define the corresponding categories yourself.

Go to the "Expense tracker" menu in the app. Then select the "not categorized" category. To allocate transactions to a category, swipe the transaction left with your finger and then click "Categorize". You are then shown the selection of the categories you have defined. If you haven’t defined any categories yet, you can create different categories depending on your requirements. For this, simply click the plus icon on the top right, choose a name and color and you have created the category.
