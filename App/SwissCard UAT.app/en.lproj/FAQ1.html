You have mobile access to the most important card details quickly and easily using the Swisscard app. You can view the following information:

  • Account status 
  • Current balance
  • Credit limit
  • Available amount 
  • Summary of transactions by quarter, month or week
  • Transaction details
  • Summary of spending ("Spending assistant"), sorted by categories

If you have several private cards from Swisscard, this information is displayed clearly for each one of your products.
